Jobs

There is no uniformity in regards to remuneration in the field of Electronics & Teleommunication Engineering. It depends heavily on the portrayal of the individual and their ability. There is no dissatisfaction that electronics and communication engineering is a promising growth-driven stream, and adequate expertise in the field qualifies the person for a broad range of entry-level openings when merged with unique skills.

Job Profile Role
Electronic Engineer Use scientific knowledge of electrons to design and develop systems that use electricity as a source of power
Verification Engineer Has to verify the design and make sure it works properly.
CAD Engineer Responsible for managing the license and EDA tools and need to integrate good interpersonal skills.
Application Engineer Responsible for maintaining interface between R&D and customers.
Design Engineer Responsible for designing and evaluating devices and procedures, performing research and implementation.
